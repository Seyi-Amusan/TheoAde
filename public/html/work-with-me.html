Website Design

I design brilliant websites that are fully optimized for conversion. Whether for your personal brand or business, a good website is a good investment, especially if you are in it for the long haul. A good website does three things: it connects with the right type of audience, leads people to your core offerings (they must be able to tell in clear terms what you do), and collects their contact details for future business relationships.

Sadly, most websites are boring billboards of information that do not serve the owner. They fail to reflect novelty, and visitors simply come, look around, remember nothing, feel overwhelmed, and leave.

Which is what we do differently…or which is where I come in:

  • • First we understand your goals for setting up the website
  • • Then we streamline your message so that you get known for a singular, important message
  • • Then we lay out a good design with a stunning, website architecture…
  • • Then we integrate relevant plug-ins to track and optimize performance…
  • • Then we refine the overall experience…
  • • Bearing in mind the three vital functions of a website…connecting, leading, & conversion

Marketing

I work with creators and entrepreneurs to design smart funnels that sell their product or service digitally.

A funnel is a sequence of steps that a prospect goes through before they buy from you. This can be as simple as your traffic— sales page—purchase, or a little more intricate as your traffic— quiz—opt-in page—email sequences—sales page—purchase. I work with you to define the best funnel for your product or service, craft the copies, and help you design a well-optimized funnel. Then we automate the process, and your only goal will be to drive traffic to the front of the funnel and watch the magic happen.
